Skeleton Conversation plugin

  1. Open the Bot by clicking the icon in lower right
  2. Click "I want to see my latest invoices"
  3. Click "Yes - get my latest invoices"
  4. Enter atleast 10 random numbers as your ID and click "Log in"
  5. Click "Authenticate" in the BankID prompt
  6. Click any of the presented choices to simulate downloading an invoice

This skeleton uses two plugins. One for handling easy log in with (a mocked) Bank ID and one for fetching and downloading invoices.

You can find the plugin code at ./src/bank-id-plugin.js and ./src/invoice-plugin.js.
